LIAD and TOP LEADER MEETING

November 1, 2019
TLC PUBLICITY
Blog, NEWS
0

40 Leaders Attend Industry Dinner and Strategy Meeting in Malaysia

In line with TLC’s advancement into the international arena, 40 senior leaders were selected to participate in the Top Leader Strategy Meeting in Malaysia to confer on the Group’s future plans and direction. In addition, the leaders also shouldered another important task of representing TLC to attend the Labuan Industry Annual Dinner (LIAD) during the 4-day trip.

Senior leaders arriving at Labuan Airport

LIAD: Where Labuan Financial Elites Come Together

The Labuan Industry Annual Dinner (LIAD) is a rendezvous of industry players at the midshore business and financial centre for information exchange and network building. The prestigious event was held on 18th October 2019 at Level 3 Convention Hall of Financial Park Labuan.

Labuan Industry Annual Dinner is held in high regards by the local business and financial community

 

TLC leaders from all over the world join in on the grand get-together

Guests hail from various international financial sectors that include banking, fund, trust, currency brokerage, wealth management and insurance. Attendance to the event is exclusively by invitation only. Therefore, TLC is deeply honoured to be invited to the celebration.

In addition, Dato’ Howard Choo, CEO of our strategic partner, IBH Investment Bank was one of the VVIPs of the evening, as he also serves as the Chairman of Labuan Investment Bank Group.

Dato’ Howard Choo (4th from left), CEO of IBH Investment Bank and Chairman of Labuan Investment Bank Group with TLC leaders

TLC’s senior leaders have benefited a great deal from the event. Apart from getting to know Labuan’s financial ecosystem up close, we were able to integrate ourselves and let local counterparts know even more about us.

Top Leader Strategy Meeting: Mapping the Future

On the third day of the trip, the team arrived in Kuala Lumpur and went straight to JW Marriott Hotel for the Top Leader Strategy Meeting. TLC’s top management freely shared and analysed the Company’s overall operations to date at the meeting, as well as formulated new directions and policies to continue propelling the Group towards the global market.

Leaders focusing on the agenda at hand

Japanese team engaging in lively discussion

TLC’s key personnel answering questions from leaders while giving constructive feedback and suggestions

As the Company’s blueprint for the future becomes clearer to the leaders, we saw an increased level of confidence among the group at the end of the trip. Now that they gain a better understanding of the Company’s development and deployment, they feel more comfortable when leading their teams.

We hope to build stronger ties and rapport with our leaders through the meeting, while working together to drive TLC towards an even more brilliant future!

Huge success for Top Leader Strategy Meeting!
